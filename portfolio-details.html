Facts about Antique

  • The province of Antique was one of the oldest political divisions in the Philippines, dating back to the Spanish colonial period.


  • The capital of Antique was San Jose de Buenavista, which was also the main center of trade and commerce in the province.


  • The province was also known for its traditional crafts, such as weaving, pottery, and metalworking.

FACT

Antique is also known for its rich history and cultural heritage, with many ancient artifacts and monuments found in the province. The Antique Rice Terraces, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an example of the province's cultural heritage, it is a traditional farming technique passed down through generations and still in use today.
